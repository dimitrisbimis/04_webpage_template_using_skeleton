I am a 1st year undergrad student at NTUA School of Architecture. This is my website for ARCH 4342 Computational Methods in Creative Arts and Sciences.

As part of this course, I’ve been exploring programming; an area I find both challenging and rewarding and I'm currently working on this website (one day it will become really impressive, I swear!).

Alongside my architectural studies, I’ve developed an interest in Photoshop and sketching. These creative outlets have become a part of my daily routine. I really enjoy engaging with these two and it is actually really fulfilling to see my skills improve in areas closely connected to both my academic journey and future professional path.
